The 8th Wonder of the World

Sigiriya is an ancient rock fortress and palace built by King Kasyapa in the 5th century. It is famous for its massive columns of rock, ancient frescoes, and the giant lion paws carved into the entrance.

Quick Travel Tips for Sigiriya:

  • Best time to climb: Early morning (7:00 AM) to avoid the heat. [cite: 8]
  • Difficulty: Moderate (1,200 steps to the summit).
  • What to bring: Water, a hat, and comfortable walking shoes. [cite: 8]

Once you reach the top, you are rewarded with 360-degree views of the surrounding jungles and water gardens below. It is a must-visit for any traveler to Sri Lanka.
